Smoot Pursuit Eye Tracking Using a Webcam

With one click to the map

Click here!

Bachelor's thesis at the University of Salzburg

My thesis seeks to develop and evaluate a proof of concept web application that implements non-calibrated smooth-pursuit eye tracking using a standard webcam.
The interface includes four buttons. You are instructed to say out loud where you are looking. When a red point appears near the button you are looking at, you should blink your eyes.
Note: the red point will not appear exactly on the button but close to it.
